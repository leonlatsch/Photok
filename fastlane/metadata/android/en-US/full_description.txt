Keep your private photos and videos truly secure. Photok is a trusted encrypted photo vault that protects your sensitive media with military-grade AES-256 encryption.

<b>Why Choose Photok?</b>

Your privacy matters. Unlike other gallery apps, Photok encrypts your photos directly on your device and only decrypts them in memory while viewing - meaning your private moments stay private, even if someone gains access to your phone.

<b>Secure & Private</b>

- Military-grade AES-256 encryption
- Photos never stored unencrypted on your device
- No cloud uploads - everything stays on your phone
- Hide the app itself for extra privacy

<b>Easy to Use</b>

- Import photos and videos with one tap
- Organize your media in custom albums
- Export photos back when you need them
- Auto-delete originals after import for peace of mind

<b>Complete Control</b>

- Create secure backups of your vault
- Share directly to Photok from other apps
- Restore your vault anytime
- 100% free, no ads, no subscriptions

<b>Open Source & Trusted</b>

Photok is completely open source, developed by security-conscious developers and trusted by thousands of users worldwide. No hidden tracking, no data collection, just pure privacy protection.

Perfect for protecting personal photos, confidential documents, or any media you want to keep away from prying eyes.
